    About Me

    I am an Associate Professor at Xi'an University of Technology. I obtained my Ph.D. in Data Science from City University of Hong Kong in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. ZHANG Zijun. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering & Automation from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019.

    Research Interests: My research focuses on time series forecasting, renewable energy, and anomaly detection.
