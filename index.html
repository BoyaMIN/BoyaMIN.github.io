    About Me

    I am a Senior Battery Management System Engineer at BYD Auto, Institute of Advanced Technology. I obtained my Master's degree in Power Engineering & Engineering Thermophysics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. GUO Liejin and Prof. LIU Maochang. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering & Technology from Southeast University in 2019.

    Research Interests: My research focuses on AI4Science, electrocatalysis for water splitting, photocatalytic CO2 reduction, advanced nanomaterials for catalysis, and battery management systems.
